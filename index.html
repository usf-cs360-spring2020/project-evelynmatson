Project Goals

With this project, I plan to create visual explorations of the current state of world happiness and other factors like inequality, that investigate what contributes to people's happiness. I hope to make visualizations that encourage users to explore the data and enable them to learn what they want to find out, rather than just showing one conclusion.

I hope to make these visualizations visually appealing and easy to explore and interact with, and I plan to use strong encodings for data that are simple and intuitive, yet convey lots of data.

Project Narrative

In this project, I will exhibit the different factors that contribute to people's happiness, and allow users to explore how these vary across countries. This includes social factors like perceived generosity or perceived social support, political factors like freedom to make life choices or perceptions of corruption, economic factors like GDP per capita. Since different people value these factors in different ways, I will give users a way to customize how much weight each is given.
