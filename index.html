Project Goals

With this project, I plan to create visual explorations of the current state of world happiness and other factors like inequality, that investigate the connection between people's happiness and other indicators of life today.

I hope to make these visualizations visually appealing and easy to explore and interact with, and I plan to use strong encodings for data that are simple and intuitive, yet convey large amounts of data.

Project Narrative

In this project, I will explore what factors contribute to people's happiness. This includes social factors like perceived generosity or perceived social support, political factors like freedom to make life choices or perceptions of corruption, economic factors like GDP per capita or amount of income inequality, and others. I hope to make a visual demonstration of what parts of a society help or hinder people's ability to live happy, fulfilled lives.
